Jammu & Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is a breathtaking region in northern India. It’s known for its snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, serene lakes, and vibrant gardens. The union territory offers spiritual sites, such as Vaishno Devi and Amarnath, along with natural beauty. From the charm of Srinagar to the adventure of Gulmarg and Pahalgam, it captivates every traveler.

                    


                        
                
            
  • Srinagar – Known for Dal Lake, houseboats, Mughal gardens, and Shikara rides..

  • Gulmarg – A popular hill station for skiing, cable car rides (Gondola), and snow adventures..

  • Pahalgam – A peaceful valley famous for Lidder River, Betaab Valley, and trekking routes..
    •     
  • Sonamarg– The “Meadow of Gold,” surrounded by glaciers, alpine meadows, and scenic trails.
    •     
  • Leh-Ladakh – A high-altitude desert with dramatic landscapes, monasteries, and bike adventures..
    •     
  • Jammu – Known for the sacred Vaishno Devi Temple and historic forts and palaces.
    •     
  • Kupwara – A lesser-known gem with stunning natural beauty and offbeat mountain views.
    •     
  • Pulwama – Famous for saffron fields, apple orchards, and tranquil countryside.
    •     
  • Hemis – Known for Hemis Monastery and the vibrant Hemis Festival.

Kerala is a beautiful state in the southwestern part of India, often referred to as “God’s Own Country” due to its stunning natural landscapes. It is famous for its peaceful backwaters, where you can enjoy houseboat cruises through calm waters surrounded by palm trees and traditional villages. The Western Ghats offer lush green hill stations like Munnar and Wayanad, ideal for trekking, tea plantation visits, and wildlife spotting.

Clothing of India

India’s clothing is as diverse as its cultures, regions, and climates. Traditional attire reflects local customs, materials, and craftsmanship, often passed down through generations. Clothing here is not just functional — it is an expression of identity, artistry, and heritage. Festivals, weddings, and ceremonies bring out the most elaborate designs, while everyday wear balances comfort with style. From handwoven fabrics to intricate embroidery, Indian clothing is a blend of tradition and modern fashion.
